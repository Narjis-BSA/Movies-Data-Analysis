Conclusions¶

At last, analyzing the data set of IMDB Movies was challanging with some limitation. Limitation were the multiple values in some fields and not having the rate of each movie. Where some movies could have multiple genres, in this case only the first genres of that field was selected which gives a false indication in the analysis.

For the the first question, it was what movie genres gain high revenue? and the answer from the graph showed that Advanture genres movies gain the highest revnue. Then comes the Fiction and Animation genres. Action genres movies ranked the sixth.

The second question was which genres is more popular? and the answer showed that Action and Advanture movies scored the highest among all genres. then the popularity dropped by 80% for Animation then Comedy movies.

In cnclusion, the Advanture genres movies has the highest popularity that is associated with high revenue. On the other hand Action movies ranked on high popularity while the revenue of such genres is significantly lower than the Advanture genres. that what led me to check the counts of each genres to check the amount of Action and Advanture movies that have been released. the surprise was that Action movies count was 1478 while the Advanture movies are almost third with only 551 released movies. that what leads to the fact that Advanture movies are the most popular and gain the highest revenue.
